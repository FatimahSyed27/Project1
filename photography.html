Mariana Trench

Have you ever heard about the Mariana Trench?

The Mariana Trench is one of the most deepest point on Earth that has been discovered by scientist. It is located in the western Pacific Ocean and measures about 2,550 km in length and 69 km in width.

It's deeper than the highest mountain and no human being has ever traveled all the way bottom!
